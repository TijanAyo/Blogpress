About Sug Press .

Sug press is an open source WebApp( all code are available on my github repo), with the purpose of aiding student get information around the school and reduce the spread of rumoured information that can be going around, so you can say killing 2 birds with one stone ;)

Why would i use Sug Press ?

To be honest the reason for creating this is because i got fed up with the rumours going around the school and because i noticed that the sug news been passed on whatsapp group and also been posted on whatsapp tv isn't reaching all the student.
Which the bring questions like why can't we use other platform like telegram, whatsapp and the likes, but the answer you question how many times have you opened your telegram of recent and would you like to get notifications blowing up you phone making you mute the whatsapp group chat, hence the creation of this platform.
So you can just come at anytime of the day check what is going on, respond(it's your choice) and exit that information is passed well enough. Plus the Sug can create polls so as to better communicate with student on any matter of there choice.
